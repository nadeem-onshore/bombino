Redesign ONLY the Home screen layout of the Bombino Express Phase 1 app to introduce clear visual hierarchy, differentiated CTAs, and a more professional, minimal, high-utility feel.

Do NOT add new features. Do NOT change Phase 1 scope. This is a layout and hierarchy correction only.

PRIMARY GOAL:
Users should immediately understand:
1) What is the PRIMARY action
2) What is the SECONDARY action
3) What requires LOGIN vs what does not
4) What Bombino stands for (without clutter)

----------------------------------
A) HOME SCREEN STRUCTURE (NEW HIERARCHY)
----------------------------------

Use a 4-zone layout instead of stacked white cards:

ZONE 1 – PRIMARY ACTION (Hero Section)
----------------------------------
This is the ONLY dominant card on the screen.

• Purpose: Track Shipment (guest allowed)
• Design:
  - Full-width hero card
  - Subtle red gradient background (very light, not loud)
  - Clear title: “Track your shipment”
  - Single input field (AWB)
  - One bold primary CTA button (red)

• Remove any secondary CTAs from this zone.
• This section must visually dominate the screen.

----------------------------------
ZONE 2 – CORE ACTIONS (Action Strip)
----------------------------------
Immediately below the hero, place TWO horizontally aligned action tiles:

• Get Rates
• Ship Now

Design rules:
- These must NOT look like the hero card
- Use icon + label style (not large cards)
- Slightly elevated, compact, rectangular tiles
- Distinct icon background colors:
  - Get Rates → muted gold / amber accent
  - Ship Now → muted green accent
- Clearly secondary to tracking, but still actionable

----------------------------------
ZONE 3 – AUTHENTICATION CTA (CLEAN & ELEGANT)
----------------------------------
Replace the current stacked login/signup block.

Design as a SINGLE compact banner:
- Text: “Sign in to manage your shipments”
- Two inline buttons:
  - Primary: Login (filled)
  - Secondary: Sign Up (outline)

Rules:
- No card container
- No heavy background
- Minimal height
- Should feel optional, not forced
- Place AFTER core actions, not before

----------------------------------
ZONE 4 – SUPPORT + TRUST (LIGHTWEIGHT)
----------------------------------

Split support and trust into TWO minimal sections:

1) Support (horizontal pills):
   - WhatsApp
   - Call Us
   - Use pill buttons, not cards
   - Soft background tint, no elevation

2) Why Bombino? (Visual strip):
   - Replace large cards with small icon + label chips
   - Use subtle background tint (very light red)
   - Max 3 items
   - Compact height

----------------------------------
B) VISUAL DIFFERENTIATION RULES
----------------------------------

1) Only ONE section may be a “card with elevation” → Hero section
2) Action tiles must be flatter and smaller
3) Support must feel lightweight and secondary
4) Trust indicators must feel informational, not clickable

----------------------------------
C) COLOR & CONTRAST USAGE
----------------------------------

• Red = Primary actions only
• White cards = Use sparingly
• Background = light neutral gray
• Icons = solid, bold, recognizable
• No more than 3 accent colors on the home screen

----------------------------------
D) SPACING & BREATHING ROOM
----------------------------------

• Increase vertical spacing between sections
• Reduce padding inside cards
• Avoid stacking multiple rounded containers back-to-back
• Let the background breathe between sections

----------------------------------
E) LOGIN / SIGNUP BEHAVIOR
----------------------------------

• Guest users:
  - See Track Shipment
  - See Get Rates
  - See Ship Now
  - See Login/Signup banner only once

• Logged-in users:
  - Login/Signup banner disappears
  - Home feels cleaner and more focused

----------------------------------
F) FINAL FEEL CHECK
----------------------------------

The Home screen must feel:
✔ Calm
✔ Focused
✔ Intentional
✔ Professional
✔ Enterprise-grade

If two elements look equally important, hierarchy has failed — simplify further.
